1. Collect Chat

Collect Chat is a no-code, conversational form/chatbot builder focused on lead capture and data collection.

Instead of a static form, Collect Chat runs a guided conversation (question-by-question) that feels like messaging — claimed benefits are higher conversions and simpler data capture (and it offers many templates for industries like real estate, education, travel, etc.).

It’s explicitly positioned as a drag-and-drop, plug-and-play chatbot for websites and WordPress.

Key features

  • Drag-and-drop builder + ready templates (50+ templates).
  • Multiple message types (questions, images, videos), email notifications, Google Sheets / Zapier integrations.
  • No-code installation (copy/paste script or WP plugin).

Best for

Small to medium sites that want to boost lead capture or replace long forms with a friendly conversational flow without building custom chat AI or paying for complex bot platforms.

Pros And Cons

Pros:

Extremely beginner-friendly, lots of templates, inexpensive/free tiers for simple use.

Cons:

It’s primarily a scripted conversational form (not a full generative AI agent); complex natural-language understanding or deep knowledge-base answering is limited compared with AI-first bots.

2. Smartsupp

Smartsupp is a live-chat platform that bundles real-time messaging, rule-based chatbots and AI helpers (for example, AI Reply Assistant / AI Shopping Assistant) so teams can combine automated answers with human handoffs.

It excels for e-commerce: chat + bot + AI suggestions live in one dashboard (also integrates with WhatsApp, Messenger and email), offers agent assist features (message suggestions, grammar/tone polishing) and has ready integrations (Shopify app, mobile apps).

Good choice if you need a hybrid setup where humans and lightweight AI collaborate to drive sales and support.

  • Live chat + AI chatbot + “Mira” AI shopping assistant for automated replies and product recommendations.
  • Multichannel inbox (chat, Messenger, WhatsApp, email), mobile apps, and analytics/agent tools.
  • WordPress, Shopify and plugin/app integrations; templates for lead gen and sales automation.

Best for

Ecommerce stores and online shops that need a hybrid approach (human + AI) to handle product questions, recommend items, and automate answers while keeping live agents in the loop.
